introduction

Today, the world is witnessing a major revolution in the field of artificial intelligence in various economic, political, cultural and educational fields. Artificial intelligence is classified as one of the modern technologies capable of creating new content and new ideas and performing complex tasks that in the past required human intervention. It also plays a major role in the field of education, teaching, applications and various educational platforms, including the ability to develop and modernize the education system, practices used in the field of education, allocating appropriate teaching methods, developing educational platforms, improving the student learning experience, creating teaching and evaluation plans, data analysis, learning effectiveness and enhancement, and other options. The conference on foreseeing the future of education in the era of artificial intelligence at the University of Hafr Al-Batin comes at a time when artificial intelligence has begun to occupy a great place and interest in the field of education. This conference is also in line with the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia's Vision 2030, which seeks to achieve development and progress in several sectors, including the education sector, which is considered a basic and important tributary of development in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.






Vision

The conference will be a scientific event that contributes to disseminating the most prominent contributions in the field of artificial intelligence and education.

Message

Towards a vision for the future of education in the era of artificial intelligence and stimulating a culture of research and innovation.

Conference Objectives

  1. Building forward-looking scenarios for the future of education in the era of artificial intelligence.
  2. Presenting recent studies and innovations in the field of artificial intelligence and education.
  3. Explaining the positive and negative effects of artificial intelligence on the elements of the educational process.
  4. Creating a scientific platform and a research vessel for knowledge sharing, exchanging experiences and developing skills on employing the latest artificial intelligence technologies in education.
  5. Reviewing international experiences in employing artificial intelligence in education and the possibility of benefiting from them.
  6. Exchanging different experiences and expertise in the conference sessions.

Conference topics

Successful international experiences in employing artificial intelligence in education

By presenting successful international experiences and practices in employing artificial intelligence in education, benefiting from them in developing education in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

Employing artificial intelligence technologies in education

Their uses enhance teaching and learning experiences, such as machine learning, language processing, data analysis, and the use of educational chatbots.

Policies and ethics of artificial intelligence in education

This regulates the employment of artificial intelligence in education, ensuring the preservation of ethical considerations such as data privacy, algorithmic bias, transparency, accountability, and societal impacts.

Preparing and professionally developing the teacher of the age of artificial intelligence

Employing AI technologies in building curricula, teaching strategies, tests, assessment methods, and self-learning motivation, while building teacher training programs based on current and future needs.

Educational management, leadership, and ensuring quality of education in the age of artificial intelligence

Activating AI in building strategic orientations, operational plans, performance indicators, governance, decision-making, and enhancing educational institution efficiency.

Curricula and teaching methods in the age of artificial intelligence

Planning for teaching, high-quality curricula, assessment tools, correcting tests, and using AI in educating diverse student groups (high achievers, disabled, children), improving efficiency and addressing educational loss.

Learning Psychology in the Age of Artificial Intelligence

Addressing psychological and social problems faced by students, providing solutions that help understand the human mind, and using AI to analyze students’ behavior and predict future actions.

Early childhood and artificial intelligence

Using AI to foster collaboration between children and computers, enhancing their knowledge and creativity, preparing them for a digital future, and improving problem-solving skills.
